Our Mission

Xolor's vision is to automate the process of diagnostics by augmenting the expertise of a radiologist for real-time defect detection and disease diagnosis.

About

We are an all-women's team from Shiv Nadar University aiming to solve the issue of diagnosis and facilitate the same through automation of a radiologist's work. We are experienced in working with ML and deep learning models, predictive analytics and front-end development. Our team is a mix of determined, smart and innovative CSE and ECE engineers.
